Account Opening

PISO Account

Intro guide for opening a PISO Account at LANDBANK Tanauan Branch.

What to prepare (intro)

  • Valid ID(s)
  • Basic personal info (address, contact number)
  • Initial deposit (as required by the product)

Suggested steps (intro)

  1. Visit the branch and request to open a PISO Account.
  2. Fill out forms and submit requirements.
  3. Make the initial deposit and confirm account details.
