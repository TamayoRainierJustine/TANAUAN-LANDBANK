Account Updating

UCPB Customers

Update your personal details (email, contact number, etc.) linked to your account.

What you can update

  • Email / mobile number
  • Address and contact details
  • Other customer profile information

Suggested process (intro)

  1. Bring valid ID and account details.
  2. Visit Tanauan Branch and request profile update/maintenance.
  3. Fill out forms and submit supporting documents.
  4. Wait for verification and confirmation.

Note: Final requirements are subject to bank policy.
