Teller Services

TELLER TRANSACTIONS

Perform over-the-counter transactions through the teller. Ensure all required forms are completed before proceeding.

Important Notice: All transactions require two (2) properly filled-out slips before going to the teller.

All clients are required to accomplish two (2) transaction slips prior to proceeding to the teller.

Core Services

DEPOSITS AND WITHDRAWAL

Standard teller transactions for account funding and cash release.

💵

Cash Deposit

Deposit cash directly to your account through the teller.

🧾

Check Deposit

Deposit checks for clearing and credit to your account.

🏧

Withdrawal

Withdraw funds from your account.

Note: Transactions exceeding ₱100,000 require additional verification.

Additional Services

PAYMENTS AND ENCASHMENT

Teller-assisted transactions for encashment and payment processing.

💳

Encashment

Encash checks over the counter.

Note: Transactions exceeding ₱100,000 require additional verification.

🏛️

BIR Payments

Pay taxes and government dues through the teller.

📞

On-call Payments

Process payments requested and verified by authorized personnel.
